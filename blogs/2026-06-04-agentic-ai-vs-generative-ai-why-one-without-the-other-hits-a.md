The article distinguishes between generative AI, which produces content like text and code, and agentic AI, which makes decisions and executes multi-step tasks. While generative systems excel at drafting and ideation, they lack the orchestration capabilities needed for real-world workflows. The most effective solutions combine both approaches: using generation for content creation while leveraging agentic architecture for task management, tool integration, and adaptive decision-making in production environments.
